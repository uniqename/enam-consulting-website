‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
How do I submit a prayer request?
Go to the Prayer Ministry section, tap "Submit Prayer Request," fill out the form with your request details, choose privacy settings (private, church family, or prayer team only), and submit. Our prayer team will be notified immediately.
Is my child's information safe?
Absolutely. We comply with COPPA and have strict safety measures including parental consent, supervised access, age-appropriate content filtering, and no unsupervised communication. Parents have full control over their child's account and data.
How do I join a live stream?
Go to the Live Streaming section when a service is active. You'll see options to watch via YouTube, Facebook, or Zoom. Choose your preferred platform and tap to join. Links are automatically updated for each service.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes, you have full control over your data. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account, or contact privacy@faithklinik.org. We'll permanently remove all your personal information within 30 days, except for anonymized usage statistics.
How do I report inappropriate content or behavior?
Use the "Report" button next to any content or message, or email privacy@faithklinik.org with details. For children's safety issues, email children@faithklinik.org immediately. All reports are reviewed within 2 hours.
Why can't I see certain features?
Some features require specific permissions (like location for event notifications) or ministry roles. Check your account settings and permissions, or contact support if you believe you should have access to additional features.

üì± Technical Support

Before Contacting Support, Try These Steps:

  1. Update the App: Check app store for updates
  2. Restart the App: Close completely and reopen
  3. Check Internet: Ensure stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection
  4. Restart Device: Power off and on your phone/tablet
  5. Clear Cache: In device settings, clear app cache

When Contacting Technical Support, Please Include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version number (found in Settings)
  • Description of the issue and when it started
  • Steps you've already tried
  • Screenshots of error messages (if any)

üì± App Setup Guide

Getting Started with Faith Klinik

  1. Download the App: Install Faith Klinik Ministries from Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  2. Create Account: Use your real name and church-registered email for verification
  3. Login Credentials: Your username will be your first name in lowercase (e.g., "john") and initial password is your name + "123" (e.g., "john123")
  4. First Login: Change your password immediately after first login in Settings
  5. Profile Setup: Complete your profile information including contact details and ministry interests
  6. Permissions: Allow notifications to stay updated with church announcements and prayer requests

üóëÔ∏è Account & Data Deletion

Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your Faith Klinik account and all associated data:

  1. Email us at privacy@faithklinik.org
  2. Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your username and registered email address
  4. We will process your request within 48 hours

Delete Specific Data

You can also request deletion of specific data types:

  • Prayer requests: Remove individual prayer submissions
  • Messages: Delete communication history
  • Profile information: Remove personal details
  • Activity data: Clear usage statistics and app interactions
  • Children's data: Complete removal of all child account information

Contact privacy@faithklinik.org with your specific deletion requests.

Data Retention Policy: After account deletion, we retain anonymized usage statistics for 90 days for security and improvement purposes. All personally identifiable information is immediately and permanently removed from our systems.

Children's Account Deletion

For children's accounts (under 13):

  • Parent/Guardian Request Required: Only parents or legal guardians can request deletion
  • Immediate Processing: Children's data deletion requests are processed within 24 hours
  • Complete Removal: All data, including supervised content and activities, is permanently deleted
  • Verification: We may require identity verification for child account deletions

Contact: children@faithklinik.org for child account deletion requests.
