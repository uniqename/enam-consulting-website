Privacy Policy

Effective: April 29, 2026
Our Commitment: NativeNest is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you stay in control of it.

1. About NativeNest

NativeNest ("we", "our", or "us") is Africa's real estate marketplace connecting buyers, renters, and sellers with verified property agents across Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Senegal, and beyond. This Privacy Policy applies to the NativeNest mobile application (iOS and Android).

By using NativeNest, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account details: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo (optional)
  • Property preferences: Search filters, saved properties, preferred locations and price ranges
  • Enquiry information: Messages sent to agents, booking requests, virtual tour requests
  • Documents: Files you upload to your personal document vault within the app
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal — we never store card numbers or full payment credentials
  • Reviews and ratings you submit for agents

Automatically Collected

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Location data (only when you actively use location-based property search; never in the background)
  • Session data: features accessed, search terms, properties viewed
  • Push notification tokens for booking alerts and agent replies
  • Crash reports and performance metrics for app stability

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your NativeNest account
  • To display relevant property listings based on your search and preference history
  • To connect you with verified property agents and facilitate enquiries
  • To process payments and issue transaction records
  • To send booking confirmations, agent replies, and app updates via push notification or email
  • To verify agent credentials and protect you from fraudulent listings
  • To comply with applicable laws and respond to legal requests
  • To improve our platform through aggregated, anonymized analytics

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only as follows:

  • Agents you enquire with: Your name and message content are shared with the agent you contact, so they can respond
  • Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay — governed by their own privacy policies
  • Supabase (database & auth): Our backend infrastructure provider, compliant with SOC 2 and GDPR
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of users

5. Multi-Currency & International Use

NativeNest supports GHS, USD, GBP, and CAD. Currency preferences and transaction records are stored in your account. We do not collect tax identification numbers or perform currency exchange — pricing displayed is for informational purposes.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). We implement industry-standard security practices including row-level security policies to ensure you can only access your own data.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, your data is removed within 30 days (see Section 9).

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information via your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request full deletion of your account and data (see Section 9)
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a common format
  • Opt-out: Disable marketing emails and push notifications in app settings at any time

8. Children's Privacy

NativeNest is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us immediately at consulting.enam@gmail.com.

9. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, visit our data deletion page or email us directly:

  • Online form: doxaandco.co/nativenest/privacy/delete-data.html
  • Email: consulting.enam@gmail.com with subject "NativeNest Data Deletion Request"

Deletion is processed within 30 days. Some data may be retained as required by law (e.g. transaction records for tax compliance).

10. Third-Party Links

Property listings may contain links to external websites. NativeNest is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of NativeNest after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
