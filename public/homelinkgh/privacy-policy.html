Privacy Policy

HomeLinkGH Mobile Application

Effective Date: April 10, 2026 | Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Our Commitment: HomeLinkGH is committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your personal information. This app is designed to connect homeowners with trusted service providers with the highest standards of data protection.

1. About HomeLinkGH

HomeLinkGH ("we", "our", or "us") is Ghana's AI-powered home services platform connecting homeowners with trusted, verified service providers for plumbing, electrical, cleaning, carpentry, and more. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the HomeLinkGH mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile data: Profile photo (optional), service preferences
  • Service address: Home or service location when booking
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Paystack and Stripe — we never store card details
  • Ratings and reviews you submit for service providers
  • Messages exchanged with service providers through the app

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Location data (only when you use location-based search features)
  • App usage data such as features accessed and session duration
  • Push notification tokens for service updates and booking alerts
  • Crash logs and performance data for app stability

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with suitable, verified service providers
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To send service confirmations, reminders, and status updates
  • To verify service provider identities and credentials
  • To enable ratings and reviews for quality assurance
  • To improve app features and user experience
  • To respond to customer support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

  • Service providers you book: Receive your name, contact details, and service address to fulfil the booking
  • Payment processors: Paystack and Stripe process payments securely under their own privacy policies
  • Cloud infrastructure: Supabase stores your data securely on encrypted servers
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by Ghanaian law or a valid court order

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Paystack — payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — international payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase / FCM — push notifications (Privacy Policy)

We do not use advertising networks, behavioral tracking, or cross-app tracking of any kind.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud platform. All data in transit is protected using HTTPS/TLS encryption. No payment card data is stored on our servers — all financial transactions are handled by Paystack (PCI-DSS compliant) and Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified).

7. Location Data

Location access is used only to help you find nearby service providers and to provide your address when booking. We do not track your location in the background. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings without affecting core app functionality.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your profile information directly in the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion via our Account Deletion page
  • Notifications: Disable push notifications through your device settings
  • Location: Revoke location access at any time in device settings

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (such as payment records for tax compliance).

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), GDPR where applicable, and Google Play and Apple App Store requirements.
