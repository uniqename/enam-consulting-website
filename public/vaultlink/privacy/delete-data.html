Account & Data Deletion Request

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes your profile, listings, and messages. Escrow and contract records are retained as required by financial regulations.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, phone, photo)
  • Storage space listings you host (if applicable)
  • VA profile and public listing (if applicable)
  • Active job postings and pending applications
  • Messages and communication history
  • App preferences, saved searches, and recently viewed items

What We Retain (Required by Law)

  • Escrow payment records and release history (7 years, financial compliance)
  • Completed VA contracts and digital service agreements (7 years)
  • Storage rental transaction records (7 years, tax compliance)
  • Records related to active disputes or legal proceedings
