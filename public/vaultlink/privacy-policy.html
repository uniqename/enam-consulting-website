Privacy Policy

Effective: April 29, 2026
Our Commitment: VaultLink handles two sensitive service categories — physical storage spaces and professional virtual work arrangements. We take data security seriously and are transparent about every piece of information we collect and why.

1. About VaultLink

VaultLink ("we", "our", or "us") operates a dual-purpose marketplace: (1) a peer-to-peer storage space platform where hosts list garages, basements, rooms, and storage units for rent, and (2) a virtual assistant marketplace where clients hire professionals for admin, social media, design, research, and other remote work. This Privacy Policy applies to the VaultLink mobile application (iOS and Android), Bundle ID: com.vaultlink.app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Role: client, storage host, or virtual assistant

Storage Platform Hosts & Renters

  • Hosts: Property address, space description, photos, square footage, pricing, availability calendar, verification documents (where required)
  • Renters: Rental history, storage preferences, payment records, move-in/out dates

Virtual Assistant Platform Clients & VAs

  • VAs: Professional bio, skills, hourly rate, portfolio links, work history, availability, ratings, escrow payment records
  • Clients: Job descriptions, posted job offers, contract terms, project history, payment records
  • Contracts: Digital service agreements generated between clients and VAs

Payment & Transaction Data

  • Transactions processed via Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal
  • Escrow payment status and release records
  • We store only transaction IDs and amounts — never full card details

Automatically Collected

  • Device type, OS, and app version
  • Location (only for storage space search — city/zip level; not continuous background tracking)
  • Usage data: features accessed, search queries, listings viewed
  • Push notification tokens
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your VaultLink account
  • To show you relevant storage listings or VA profiles based on your search and location
  • To facilitate rentals, work engagements, and digital contracts between parties
  • To process payments and manage escrow funds securely
  • To send booking confirmations, contract updates, payment releases, and app notifications
  • To verify host and VA identities and protect the marketplace from fraud
  • To display your public VA or host profile to potential clients or renters
  • To resolve disputes between parties using contract and communication records
  • To improve VaultLink through anonymized, aggregated analytics
  • To comply with applicable financial and employment regulations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your data. Sharing is limited to:

  • Platform counterparties: When you book a storage space or hire a VA, relevant booking details and contact info are shared between the two parties
  • Public profiles: VA and host profiles (name, photo, service type, rating, pricing) are visible to all app users in the discovery section
  • Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay — governed by their own privacy policies
  • Supabase: Backend infrastructure provider, SOC 2 compliant
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data when required by law or to protect user safety

5. Escrow & Financial Data

VaultLink uses an escrow model for VA contracts. Payment is held in escrow until the client approves work delivery. Escrow records, including payment amounts, release dates, and involved parties, are retained for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with financial regulations. This data is not deleted even if you delete your account.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase with encryption at rest and TLS 1.2+ in transit. Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data. Property addresses of storage listings are only shared with confirmed renters after payment, not publicly visible.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a complete copy of your data
  • Correction: Update your information in Profile settings at any time
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (see Section 9); escrow and contract records are retained as required by law
  • Portability: Request a JSON export of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Disable location access and notifications in your device settings at any time

8. Children's Privacy

VaultLink is designed for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact us at consulting.enam@gmail.com if you believe a minor has registered.

9. Data Deletion

To request account and data deletion:

  • Online form: doxaandco.co/vaultlink/privacy/delete-data.html
  • Email: consulting.enam@gmail.com — subject: "VaultLink Data Deletion Request"

Standard profile and usage data is deleted within 30 days. Escrow, contract, and transaction records are retained as required by applicable financial law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via app notification or email before taking effect. Continued use of VaultLink constitutes acceptance.
