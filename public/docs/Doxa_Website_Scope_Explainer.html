Website Packages — Scope Explainer

What each deliverable means and what you actually receive

Credibility
Credibility Package
$3,500
Deliverable Tier What it includes
Brand Direction & Visual Identity Guidance Credibility+ A short creative direction session (usually 1–2 calls) to align on colour palette, typography, and overall visual tone. You receive a reference sheet with font choices, hex codes, and a brief direction summary. No full brand identity is produced at this tier — that starts at Growth.
Domain Setup & Configuration Credibility+ Purchasing or connecting an existing custom domain (e.g. yourname.com) to your hosting account. Includes DNS configuration, SSL certificate activation, and basic redirect setup (www → non-www or vice versa). Domain registration fees are the client's responsibility.
Shared Hosting Setup & Configuration Credibility Provisioning a shared hosting account (e.g. Hostinger, SiteGround), connecting it to the domain, and configuring the server environment for the site to run reliably. Monthly hosting cost is the client's responsibility after handoff.
Professional Website — up to 5 pages Credibility Design and build of up to five pages: typically Home, About, Services, Contact, and one additional page (e.g. FAQ or a single service detail). Each page is designed to your brand direction. Additional pages are available at $350 per page.
Mobile & Tablet Optimisation Credibility+ The site is built responsively so layout, text, and navigation work correctly on phones, tablets, and desktops. Tested across common screen sizes and major browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox).
Consultation Booking Integration Credibility+ A calendar booking widget (Calendly, Cal.com, or similar) embedded on your site so visitors can schedule calls without back-and-forth email. Includes setup of your availability rules and basic confirmation messaging.
Contact Form Credibility A simple contact form with name, email, and message fields. Submissions are routed to your inbox. Includes spam protection (reCAPTCHA or honeypot) and a confirmation message to the sender.
Basic SEO Foundation Credibility Each page is configured with a unique title tag, meta description, and correct heading hierarchy (H1, H2). Your site is submitted to Google Search Console and a sitemap.xml is generated. This ensures Google can discover and index your pages — ongoing ranking depends on content.
Basic Security & Performance Setup Credibility SSL/HTTPS enabled, security headers configured, image optimisation applied, and caching set up. Your site will load quickly for first-time visitors and earn a clean security posture. Does not include a Web Application Firewall (WAF) or CDN — those are included in Growth+.

Timeline: 2–3 weeks from kickoff. Best for: Solo consultants and professionals who need a clean, credible online home.
