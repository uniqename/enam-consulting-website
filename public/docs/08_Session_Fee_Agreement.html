Fee Credit Policy

The session fee is a commitment fee, not a service charge. If the Client proceeds to a signed engagement within 30 days of the session, the full session fee is credited toward the first invoice of that engagement. No deduction is made — it is applied dollar-for-dollar.

  • Strategy session ($350) → credited toward Enterprise GRC engagement
  • MVP session ($500) → credited toward MVP development contract
  • AI Advisory session ($250) → credited toward AI Readiness Audit or Build engagement

The fee exists for one reason: Paid sessions attract clients who are serious about solving a real problem. Free discovery calls attract people who are still deciding if they have one. The session fee protects both parties' time.

Non-Refund & Cancellation Policy

The session fee is non-refundable under the following circumstances:

  1. Client cancels within 24 hours of the scheduled session — no refund, no credit. The slot is lost.
  2. Client does not attend (no-show) — no refund. A new session fee is required to reschedule.
  3. Client attends but does not proceed to an engagement — the fee is earned. No refund is issued. This is the primary purpose of the policy: Enam's preparation, research, and time have value regardless of whether the Client moves forward.

The session fee is refundable only if:

  1. Doxa & Co cancels the session with less than 24 hours notice and cannot reschedule within 7 business days.
  2. A scheduling error on Doxa & Co's side results in a confirmed time that was not actually available.

Reschedule Policy

The Client may reschedule without penalty if they provide at least 24 hours' notice before the scheduled session. Only one reschedule per booking is permitted. A second reschedule requires a new booking and a new session fee.

What the Session Includes

Each paid session includes:

  • Pre-session research: review of any materials, URLs, or context provided by the Client
  • The scheduled video or phone consultation (duration per session type)
  • A written follow-up summary with key findings, recommendations, and proposed next steps, delivered within 2 business days

The session does not include deliverables, documentation, code, or implementation work. Those are covered under a separate Statement of Work.

No Engagement Guarantee

Booking and completing a paid session does not obligate either party to enter into a further engagement. Doxa & Co reserves the right to decline to take on a client after a session if the fit is not right. The session fee is not a deposit on future services.
