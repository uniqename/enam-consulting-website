Pre-Call Research

  • Review their LinkedIn / website — understand their current role and context
  • Note company size and stage (startup / scaling / enterprise)
  • Identify what session type they booked and their stated goal
  • Pull any prior notes if they have engaged with Doxa & Co before

Discovery Questions

Situation

  • What does your product/team look like right now — what are you working with?
  • What is working well, and where does it feel stuck or unclear?

Pain

  • What made you reach out now — what triggered this?
  • What have you tried already? What happened?
  • What does this problem cost you if it is not resolved in the next 90 days?

Implication

  • Who else is impacted by this — investors, team, customers?
  • What happens to the roadmap / launch timeline if this is not fixed?

Need

  • Ideally, what would "solved" look like for you?
  • What would make this engagement a clear win three months from now?
