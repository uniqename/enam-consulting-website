Pre-Call Research (complete before the call)

  • Review their LinkedIn / website — understand their current role and context
  • Note company size and stage (startup / scaling / enterprise)
  • Identify what session type they booked and their stated goal
  • Pull any prior notes if they have engaged with Doxa & Co before

Discovery Questions (use as a guide, not a script)

Situation

  1. What does your product/team look like right now — what are you working with?
  2. What is working well, and where does it feel stuck or unclear?

Pain

  1. What made you reach out now — what triggered this?
  2. What have you tried already? What happened?
  3. What does this problem cost you if it is not resolved in the next 90 days?

Implication

  1. Who else is impacted by this — investors, team, customers?
  2. What happens to the roadmap / launch timeline if this is not fixed?

Need

  1. Ideally, what would "solved" look like for you?
  2. What would make this engagement a clear win three months from now?
