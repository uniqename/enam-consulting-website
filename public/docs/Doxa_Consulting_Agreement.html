2. Term

3. Compensation & Payment

  • The Client agrees to pay Consultant the fees outlined in the Statement of Work or invoice.
  • Project-based work: 50% due upon signing; 50% due upon delivery or the agreed milestone date.
  • Retainer engagements: Invoiced on the 1st of each month. Payment due within 7 days.
  • Strategy session fees are non-refundable but credited toward any paid project initiated within 60 days.
  • Invoices unpaid after 15 days incur a 1.5% monthly late fee.
  • Accepted payment methods: ACH transfer, Zelle, PayPal, or check. Wire available upon request.

4. Independent Contractor

Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of Client. Consultant is solely responsible for all taxes, insurance, and benefits related to compensation received under this Agreement.

5. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep the other's Confidential Information strictly confidential and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent. "Confidential Information" includes business plans, product data, client lists, financial information, and any materials marked confidential. This obligation survives termination for two (2) years.

6. Intellectual Property

All work product created by Consultant specifically for Client under this Agreement shall, upon full payment, become the property of Client, except that:

  • Consultant retains rights to all pre-existing tools, frameworks, templates, and methodologies used to create the Work Product.
  • Consultant may reference the engagement in general terms in a portfolio or case study without disclosing confidential Client information, unless Client requests otherwise in writing.

7. Termination

  • Either party may terminate this Agreement with 14 days written notice.
  • Client is responsible for fees for all work completed or in-progress as of the termination date.
  • Retainer engagements: the current month's retainer is non-refundable upon termination.
  • Consultant reserves the right to terminate immediately for non-payment or conduct that violates this Agreement.

8. Limitation of Liability

Consultant's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the three (3) months preceding the claim. Consultant is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

9. Dispute Resolution

The parties agree to attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation before pursuing legal action. If unresolved, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Ohio.

10. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with any Statements of Work or Exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements relating to the subject matter herein.
