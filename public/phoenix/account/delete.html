Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

Phoenix International School is committed to respecting your privacy and data rights. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our application, please follow the instructions below.

Account Deletion Methods

You can request account deletion through any of the following methods:

1. In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Phoenix International School app
  2. Go to Settings or Account Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account" or "Privacy Settings"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion
  5. Your account will be deleted within 30 days

2. Email Request

Send an email to privacy@phoenixintlschool.edu.gh with:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Statement: "I request deletion of my account and all associated data"

We will process your request within 30 days of receipt.

3. Contact Form

Use the contact form on our website at https://phoenixintlschool.edu.gh and select "Account Deletion" as your request type.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated from the application
  • All personal data associated with your account will be deleted from our servers
  • You will not be able to log in or access any services
  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • The process typically completes within 30 days

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account access is revoked
  • Within 24 hours: Personal information is marked for deletion
  • Within 30 days: All data is permanently removed from our systems

What Data is Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your name, email address, and contact information
  • Profile information and settings
  • Any personal data you submitted through the app
  • Communication history within the app
  • Academic or enrollment records associated with your account
  • Usage logs and activity history

Important Notes

  • Deletion requests from minors must be submitted by a parent or legal guardian
  • We may retain anonymized data for statistical and analytical purposes
  • Some data may be retained for legal or compliance reasons
  • Backups may take additional time to be cleared

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or your privacy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@phoenixintlschool.edu.gh

Website: https://phoenixintlschool.edu.gh

Phone: Available through our website contact form

⚠ Warning: Account deletion is permanent. Please ensure you have downloaded any important data before requesting deletion, as it cannot be recovered afterward.
