Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Phoenix International School ("we", "our", "us", or "School") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our students, parents, staff, and users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Student or staff identification number
  • Profile picture (optional)

2.2 Educational Information

  • Academic records and grades
  • Attendance records
  • Class assignments and progress
  • Exam results and assessments
  • Enrollment information
  • Payment and billing records

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information (type, OS, model)
  • IP address and device ID
  • App usage analytics and interaction logs
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Location data (when explicitly enabled)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our educational services and application
  • Manage student enrollment and academic records
  • Communicate with students, parents, and staff
  • Process payments and billing inquiries
  • Generate reports and academic assessments
  • Improve our services and application functionality
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our systems
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

When you explicitly authorize us to share information with third-party service providers or parents/guardians

4.2 Service Providers

With trusted partners who help us operate our services (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting providers) subject to strict confidentiality agreements

4.3 Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or government request to protect legal rights or safety

4.4 School Operations

With authorized school staff members who need access to student information for educational purposes

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure password hashing and storage
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Staff training on data protection and confidentiality
  • Monitoring and logging of system access

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

7. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please visit our Account Deletion page for detailed instructions. You can also contact us directly at the email below.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you request account deletion, data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where:

  • Retention is required by law or regulation
  • Data is needed for academic accreditation records
  • Data has been anonymized for statistical purposes

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for educational purposes and may be used by minors. Parents and guardians are responsible for supervising their children's use of our application. Parents may request to view, update, or delete their child's information at any time.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our application and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
