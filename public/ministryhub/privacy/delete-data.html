Account & Data Deletion Request

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes your profile, booking history, and messages. Completed contract records are retained as required by law.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, phone, photo, bio)
  • Vendor profile and public listing (if applicable)
  • Pending bookings and enquiries
  • Messages with clients or vendors
  • App preferences and notification settings
  • Saved vendor favorites

What We Retain (Required by Law)

  • Completed contract records and digital agreements (7 years, commercial record-keeping compliance)
  • Transaction and payment history (7 years, tax and financial compliance)
  • Records related to active legal disputes
