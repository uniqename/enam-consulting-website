Privacy Policy

Effective: April 29, 2026
Our Commitment: MinistryHub respects the trust you place in us as a faith-based platform. This policy is straightforward — we collect only what we need, we protect it diligently, and we never sell your data.

1. About MinistryHub

MinistryHub ("we", "our", or "us") is a marketplace for ministry and church events, connecting event organizers with verified speakers, musicians, worship leaders, photographers, videographers, AV teams, and event planners. This Privacy Policy applies to the MinistryHub mobile application (iOS and Android) with Bundle ID: io.ministryhub.app.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account details: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo (optional)
  • Role-specific profile: For vendors — service type (speaker, musician, photographer, AV, etc.), bio, pricing, availability, portfolio links, and ratings; for clients — church or organization name, event history
  • Booking information: Event name, date, location, service requirements, agreed pricing
  • Contract data: Digital contracts generated between clients and vendors through the app
  • Messages: Communication between clients and vendors via in-app messaging
  • Payment information: Processed via Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal — payment credentials are handled entirely by these providers; we store only transaction IDs and amounts
  • Reviews and ratings you submit for vendors or clients

Automatically Collected

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Location data (only when you search for nearby vendors; not tracked in background)
  • App usage data: features used, search terms, booking patterns
  • Push notification tokens for booking updates and messages
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your MinistryHub account
  • To match event organizers with qualified, verified ministry vendors
  • To facilitate bookings, contracts, and digital agreements between parties
  • To process payments and maintain transaction records
  • To send booking confirmations, payment receipts, and scheduling reminders
  • To verify vendor credentials and protect the integrity of the marketplace
  • To display your public vendor profile to potential clients (if you are a vendor)
  • To resolve disputes between clients and vendors
  • To improve MinistryHub through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • To comply with applicable laws and financial regulations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

  • Vendors you book / clients who book you: Name, message content, and booking details are shared between the parties to facilitate the engagement
  • Public vendor profiles: If you register as a vendor, your name, service type, ratings, pricing, and bio are publicly visible in the app's discovery section
  • Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay — each governed by their own privacy policy
  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure, SOC 2 compliant
  • Legal obligations: We may share data when required by applicable law or court order

5. Vendor Public Profiles

As a service vendor on MinistryHub, your name, profile photo, service category, city/state, pricing range, and aggregate rating are publicly visible to any app user. You can edit or remove your vendor profile at any time through Profile → Edit Profile.

6. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Row-level security policies ensure each user can only access their own data. Contracts and booking records are retained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with commercial record-keeping requirements.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your profile information at any time in app settings
  • Deletion: Request full account and data deletion (see Section 9); note that completed contract records may be retained for legal compliance
  • Portability: Request a data export in JSON or CSV format
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications and marketing emails in app settings

8. Children's Privacy

MinistryHub is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If a minor has created an account, contact us at consulting.enam@gmail.com for immediate removal.

9. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

  • Online form: doxaandco.co/ministryhub/privacy/delete-data.html
  • Email: consulting.enam@gmail.com — subject: "MinistryHub Data Deletion Request"

Requests are processed within 30 days. Completed contract and transaction records may be retained as required by law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you via in-app notification or email before significant changes take effect. Continued use of MinistryHub after updates constitutes acceptance.
